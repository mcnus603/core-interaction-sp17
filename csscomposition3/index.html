The Colors of A Place

In order to look at how color scheme plays out in different places, I found photographs that I took my hometown, State College, Pennsylvania as well as photographs from my time in New York City. Once I gathered my photographs, I extracted the dominant color and compiled those colors. Below you can click on the place you want to look at further.
